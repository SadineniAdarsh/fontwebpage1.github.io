Research on the "Lobster" font:

  • Lobster is a script typeface designed by Pablo Impallari and released in 2010.
  • It is a bold and flowing font that resembles hand-lettered calligraphy.
  • The font's design is inspired by the work of lettering artist Ken Barber.
  • Lobster has gained popularity for its stylish and elegant appearance, making it suitable for various design projects.
